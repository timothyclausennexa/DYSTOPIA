How to Play

The goal of DYSTOPIA is to be the last player standing. You only live once per game - there is no respawn!

2D PUBG

If you've played other battle royale games like PUBG, Fortnite or H1Z1, then you're already halfway there! Think of DYSTOPIA as 2D PUBG (with slightly less desync and more chicken).

Loot and Kill

You'll begin the game with no items other than a simple backpack. Move around the map to find loot: weapons, ammo, scopes, and medical items. Eliminate other players and you can take their loot!

Red = Bad!

Players aren't the only thing that can hurt you. The deadly red zone will move in from the sides of the map and deal increasingly greater damage if you stand in it. Keep an eye on the map and stay safe.

What's New!

October 1st, 2025

DYSTOPIA ETERNAL - THE ETERNAL WAR BEGINS

The world has changed forever. DYSTOPIA is now a persistent battlefield where your actions have permanent consequences.

Choose your faction wisely - Red Empire, Blue Coalition, Green Alliance, Yellow Syndicate, or Purple Order. Your choice is locked for 3 months!

Fight alongside your faction in real-time battles. Build bases, control territories, and wage eternal war. The world never resets.

Create your account and join the eternal conflict. Your faction needs you!

September 9th, 2025

Preparing for the Eternal War

Major updates are coming to DYSTOPIA. The battlefield is being transformed into a persistent world where factions will battle for eternal supremacy.

Stay tuned for the biggest update in DYSTOPIA history!

July 23, 2025

New Leadership

50v50 is back for another round, and getting straight to fighting is easier than ever: select guns are now bundled with ammo.

Additionally, if a team leader fails to fire their flare soon after being promoted, it will now be auto-fired.

A new role, the Captain, has joined the ranks, but promotion to this role is not as easy as it seems. Lone Survivrs have also picked up a new perk: AP Rounds.

Enjoy a selection of role buffs and new gun world images. Let us know your thoughts by hopping into our Discord server!

May 19, 2025

Class-ified Logs

Project COBALT has begun once again on the Island! Pick from one of six classes and spawn in with special perks, then find class-pods to gather unique class loot.

Be nimble and stealthy as the Scout, unleash firepower as the Assault, or stand your ground as the deflective Tank. Team up with friends and form the perfect strategy, the group combinations are endless!

Accounts have also returned! Personalize them with a unique name and emoji, and have the ability to track and view your stats -- even gamemode specific! You can also view the leaderboards.

Need to assemble a classy squad? Make sure to join our Discord server to form the perfect team.

April 01, 2025

Boiled potato

As spring continues to progress on the island, so does the regrowth of vegetation. PARMA's FSTMS division have once again started up more testing on the fan-favorite potatoes.

Don't worry, there's plenty to go around, and is that…. Is that… An egg?

March 15, 2025

Spring Hunting

Spring has sprung once again on the island, enjoy the cherry trees! And it doesn't come alone, woods mode is also back to crown more kings!

We also made a few adjustments and balancing changes to the base game. Check the changelog.

January 27, 2025

Desert Rain

PARMA supplies have reached critical levels again, but this time, they've also got way too much sand! It's coarse and rough and irritating and it's getting everywhere. For a limited time only, squad mode will once more feature massively increased flare gun spawns.

This time around, you'll have a few new weapons for the final shootout at Ranchito Pollo. The Model 94 lever-action repeating rifle, chambered in .45 ACP, fires a speedy, accurate and decently powerful bullet.

It's companion gun is the Peacemaker revolver, also chambered in .45 ACP. The Peacemaker is deadly accurate when tap-firing, but it's real strength shows when you hold down fire and "fan the hammer." All remaining rounds in the revolver will empty at an insane pace, albeit, with a steep accuracy penalty.

January 17, 2025

Incursion recursion

50 vs 50 has arrived. To commemorate one of the bloodiest days in Island history, we're holding a field day (or week) of massive team versus team battles.

Join up alone or with a squad, and you'll be drafted into the Red Army or the Blue Group. Once both teams have locked in, one Leader will be randomly chosen for each side. Leader get a variety of perks, including a new gun and a new melee weapon! Leader pings are also visible and audible to every member of your team.

The enemy team isn't the only new danger in 50v50. Strike aircraft have been tasked with making frequent air strikes and you do not want to be caught in the bombing zone. Head indoors for relative safety, or attempt your best safety dance as the bombs fall around you.

Will you rout the Red Army with a devastating flanking maneuver? Or will the Blue Group be crushed under the weight of a decisive pincer attack? Tell us your best war story on our Discord server
